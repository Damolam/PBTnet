CGTEF 2025 REGISTRATION

We welcome Academicians, Entrepreneurs, Investors, Civil Society, Public Sector Representatives, NGOs and the Business World at large to participate in the forthcoming Conference on Global Trade and Export Finance (CGTEF 2025). Registration for the conference is free, courtesy of the conference organizers and sponsors. Registration includes:

  • Access to Sessions
  • Delegate Satchel including Program
  • Relevance, currency, appropriateness and significance of the abstract/issue/problem to the Conference theme
  • Tea Breaks & Lunches
  • Welcome Reception
  • Gala Conference Dinner
  • Excursion

Participation

Delegates can participate individually or in groups with a maximum of five (5) persons forming a group. Participation is strictly by invitation; however, recommendations can be made to other colleagues who have the interest to attend the conference.

Terms & Conditions

The following terms and conditions set out are the legal rules applicable to every CGTEF 2025 delegate. Participants shall be bound by these terms and conditions and are advised to read and understand these terms carefully before registering. The organizing committee will not accept any conditions contradictory to or deviant from these general terms and conditions.

  • To be eligible to participate in the forum, attendees must be at least 18 years old.
  • Attendees may be asked to present an official identity card stating their age at the time for accreditation.
  • The conference organizing committee reserves the right to refuse any registration if the maximum number of delegates is exceeded.
  • Registered delegates are responsible for the cost of their hotel accommodation in Jacksonville, Florida.
  • Foreign attendees are expected to honor the terms of their VISA by returning home when due
  • All delegates' details will be kept on our records, we will not share your details with anyone and we will not contact you about anything other than the event you attend unless you have indicated otherwise. This information will be discarded a year after the conference.
  • Registration will not be complete until all the requirements for participation has been fulfilled by the delegate.
  • It is imperative for all delegates to observe relevant security procedures during the period of the conference.
  • Be sure to have your delegate badge on at all times for easy identification.

To complete your registration, Click the registration form link to download the form. Read through the form and complete accurately. Filled registration forms should be submitted along with scanned copies of International Passport (data page) and hotel booking confirmation receipt to the conference secretariat's office via email

Note that delegate registration will not be confirmed without providing the above mentioned documents. Upon registration, a confirmation email will be sent to you, after which processing of travelling documents will commence.
