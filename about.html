Private Business Trade Network

The Private Business Trade Network (PBTNet) is an organisation dedicated to the promotion of international business and trade among professionals around the world.

Through the organisation of different international events, it brings together institutions, bodies and organisations from different countries of the world for discussion and cooperation.

PBTNet's Mission:

PBTNet's Mission is to promote and enhance the dialogue in business development among devoted professionals and institutions through:

  • Promotion of best practice standards in the service of international education.
  • The facilitation of relevant forums, training and information exchange.
  • Creation and dissemination of knowledge; exert an influence in public policy.
  • Production of publications used as a database document for research works, projects and innovation activities held on the international education field.

PBTNet believes that this is best achieved through international cooperation and promotes the development of closer links among relevant educational institutions and individuals around the world.

PBTNet supports that such international cooperation can help countries learn from each other and promotes the dissemination of scientific and technological activities.

PBTNet intends to achieve the mentioned objectives and get an international visibility by the organisation of international conferences and by interacting with educational public and private organisms from all parts of the world.
